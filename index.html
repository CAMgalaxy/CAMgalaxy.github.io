concepts:

  • to create: click on the area of the white screen
  • to change: click on the drawn concept and you can change the following properties:
  • Text: content of the concept
  • Type: Emotional attribution of the concept
  • Strength: Strength of the emotional attribution
  • Comment: further explanation
  • to delete: click on the drawn concept and press 'Remove' on the keyboard or use the delete button

    • conncetions:

  • to create: click on two drawn concepts
  • to change: click on the drawn connection and you can change the following properties:
  • Type of Link: positive support (solid line) or negative support (dashed line) between the two connected concepts
  • Strength: Strength of the connection
  • Direction of Influence:
  • sync_alt Concepts influence each other
  • trending_flat Concepts influence each other in a one-sided direction from left to right.
  • trending_flat Concepts influence each other in a one-sided direction from right to left.
  • to delete: click on the drawn concept and press 'Remove' on the keyboard or use the delete button
